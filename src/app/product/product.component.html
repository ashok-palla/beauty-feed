Active ingredients

HYDROLYZED FISH COLLAGEN TYPE 1 - Type 1 collagen is the most represented protein in the body, accounting for 80% of the dermis. Collagen is an integral part of the skin, hair, nails and articular cartilage. MSM - Organic sulfur is one of the components of collagen. Because of its effects on the body, it is often called the mineral of beauty.

BIOTIN - Contributes to maintaining healthy skin and hair.
